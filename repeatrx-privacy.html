Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

RepeatRx ("the app") is developed by Barry Rose. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Data We Collect

RepeatRx stores the following information that you provide:

  • Your full name, date of birth, phone number, email address, and home address
  • Your GP practice name, email address, and phone number
  • Your medication names, dosages, forms, and frequencies
  • A history of prescription requests you have sent
  • SMTP email credentials (host, port, username, password)
  • A PIN code (stored as a one-way hash) and biometric preference

2. How Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device only. RepeatRx does not operate any servers, does not send your data to any third-party service, and does not use cloud storage or analytics.

  • Personal details and medication data are stored using Apple's SwiftData framework on your device
  • Email credentials and your PIN hash are stored in the iOS Keychain, which is encrypted by the operating system
  • App preferences are stored in the device's local UserDefaults

3. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used solely to:

  • Compose and send prescription request emails to your GP practice via your own email account
  • Display a history of requests you have sent
  • Populate your personal details into email sign-offs where you have enabled this

RepeatRx does not use your data for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any purpose beyond the above.

4. Email Sending

When you send a prescription request, the app connects directly to your configured SMTP email server using credentials you provide. No email content passes through any RepeatRx infrastructure. You are sending email directly from your own account.

5. NHS Directory Search

When searching for a GP practice, the app makes a request to the publicly available NHS Organisation Data Service (ODS) API at directory.spineservices.nhs.uk. No personal data is sent in these requests — only the search term you enter (practice name or postcode).

6. Biometric Data

If you enable Face ID or Touch ID, authentication is handled entirely by iOS using Apple's LocalAuthentication framework. The app never has access to your biometric data.

7. Data Sharing

RepeatRx does not share your data with any third party. Your data never leaves your device except as part of emails you explicitly choose to send.

8. Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time from within the app:

  • Settings → Clear Data — delete individual categories of data
  • Settings → Clear Data → Reset Application — removes all data, credentials, and your PIN, returning the app to its initial state
  • Deleting the app from your device removes all locally stored data

9. Children's Privacy

RepeatRx is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
