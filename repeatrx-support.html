RepeatRx Support

Version 1.2.1

RepeatRx is a prescription management app for iOS that helps you and your family manage repeat and non-repeat medications, and send requests directly to your GP practice.

Getting Started

  • Tap Patients and press + to add your first patient
  • Fill in personal details, your GP practice name and email address, then add your medications
  • Go to Request, select the medications you need, and tap Send Prescription Request

Sending Emails

RepeatRx supports two methods for sending prescription requests:

  • Default Mail App — opens your device's default mail app (e.g. Mail, Spark, Gmail) with the email pre-composed. No setup required, but delivery is not confirmed by the app.
  • SMTP (Direct) — sends directly from your own email account using SMTP credentials. Configure this in Settings → Email Settings.

Multiple Patients

RepeatRx supports multiple patients. Each patient has their own medications, GP practice details, email preferences, and request history. Add additional patients from the Patients tab by tapping +.

PIN Protection

You can protect the app with a PIN or Face ID / Touch ID. Set this up in Settings → Security. If you forget your PIN, you can reset the app from Settings → Reset Application, though this will remove all data.

Subscription

RepeatRx is available with a 7-day free trial, then £0.99/year. Your subscription is managed through your Apple ID. To cancel, go to iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. To restore a previous purchase on a new device, tap Restore Purchase on the subscription screen.

Resetting the App

To remove all data and start fresh, go to Settings → Reset Application. This permanently deletes all patients, medications, history, and your PIN. This cannot be undone.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is the Send button greyed out?
You need at least one medication selected and your GP practice name and email address must be set up in the patient's Medical Practice section.

Can I use this for more than one person?
Yes — add each person as a separate patient from the Patients tab.

Does the app confirm my email was received?
When using the Default Mail App method, delivery is handled by your mail app and RepeatRx cannot confirm receipt. When using SMTP, a successful send is confirmed within the app.

Where is my data stored?
All data is stored locally on your device only. RepeatRx does not use any servers or cloud storage. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
